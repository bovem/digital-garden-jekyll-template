Let $g: A \rightarrow B$ and $f: B \rightarrow C$. Then composition (denoted as $f \circ g$) of both functions for all $a \in A$ is
$$(f \circ g)(a)= f(g(a))$$

[[Range]] of function $g$ has to be a [[Subsets]] of [[Domain]] of function $f$.
